Comment: Think you don't need backups. Think you'll set up backups later. Think you'll remember to always perform manual backups because you remembered today. Think a redundant RAID array is a replacement for backups.

Do your backups people. Go now and think of everything that will make life hard if you lose or can be lost forever if gone now and setup a backup for it. Your family photos. Your school projects. If you're into tinkering and customising every aspect of your system, backup your /etc, /home, perhaps also your package lists or package manager state (apt/dpkg, pacman, etc..).

If you have backups, check their status.
