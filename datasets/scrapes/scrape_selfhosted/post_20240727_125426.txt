Comment 3:
  Author: 1WeekNotice
  Comment: Just want to clarify something 

Synology is a company that offers products such as machines that have storage management, NAS and selfhosting service capabilities. 

With their products/machines they offer free of charge applications. Such as a Synology Photos which allows you to backup/ transfer your photos to their product/machine (that you will buy)


Nextcloud is a FOSS (free open source software) that you can install on any machine. 

Both are good options for yourself but the clarification that I wanted to provide is, Synology you need to purchase their product/machine before you can use their applications (as their application is closed source)

Nextcloud can be run on any hardware you currently have available. This also includes Synology hardware/ their product if you decide to purchase it. As in you aren't tied to Synology applications if you purchase a Synology device but most people use it because it comes free of change with their products and provides and all in one solution/ plug and play experience. 

&gt;Do you know which is more maintenance free?

Synology is more maintenance free if you decide to purchase their product. But of course this comes at a premium cost. Not saying that is a bad thing, just stating the fact of what you are paying for which is a plug and play experience with good support. 

I personally like to build my own machine/ purchase cheaper hardware but that is because I like to maintain my own systems. And understand I'm saving money but with the trade off of my time. 

Hope that helps.
