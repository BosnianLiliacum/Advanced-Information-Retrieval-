To check what type of motherboard you have measure it. [Here](https://storage.tweakreviews.com/Motherboard/Motherboards%20demystified:%205%20form%20factors%20for%20every%20need/motherboard-form-factor-size-sheet.jpg) is a chart you can use to see what size it is. 

As for my quick way to check, you can look at the number of PCIE slots it has and if you have the same or less than the number your case has it will fit. Your mobo has 4 and the case has 5 openings so you will be fine with that case.

As for other cases, have you looked at any of JONSBO cases? They have a lot of options, and some specifically for NAS designs.
