&lt;SOLVED!&gt; 

After I reseated all components in the machine and cleaned everything up, swapped wires and such, the problem continued, but this time it was calling out "sdf" instead.   I had moved the drives in the array around.  I removed all the drives from the array and BOOM!  Worked faster than you can imagine.  After I got Proxmox installed, I installed TrueNAS and pushed in all the drives.   Got that back online and now I'm dealing with one troubled 12TB drive.
