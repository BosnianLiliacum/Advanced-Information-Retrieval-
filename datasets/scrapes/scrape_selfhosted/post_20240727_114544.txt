Post Content:
I use several browsers between work and home and like to have them synced so I can find websites I've searched for in a browser when I don't have access to it or access my bookmarks.

At work I have to use Google Chrome and wanted to have an alternative to Google Chrome Sync that I could self-host on my own, that would also work with any major browser and search engine (at home I use Firefox, for example).

So I'm building an open source cross-browser manager for browsing activity ([Github link](https://github.com/unsearchapp/unsearch)). I would love to hear your thoughts on this concept, and if you have any questions or suggestions, please share them.
