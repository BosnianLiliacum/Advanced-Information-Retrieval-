Comment 3:
  Author: Fresh_Feesh
  Comment: IMO, you don't need all the various connections. You could, for instance, have a single connection/callout for your docker containers pointing to the Raspberry Pi rather than each service having its own. I think it's much cleaner having different line types to represent physical versus logical networking as well.

You include the 172.x IPs for several containers, but don't specify what your local network IP range (subnet, et cetera) includes.

When I think of network documentation, I want someone who sees it to easily and clearly understand the whole scope and scale of my network, if for instance I was hit by a bus and couldn't explain it to them.
