Do you require transcoding? You can disable transcoding. 

Not sure if this is the "best way" but it is a method to force the direct stream. You can also ask the [jellyfin forms](https://forum.jellyfin.org/) as they are no longer on reddit

One of the ways to disable transcoding per user is

- go into admin panel
- go to under users and select a user
- in the certain user settings de-select "allow video playback that requires transcoding"
- in the certain user settings de-select "allow audio playback that requires transcoding

This will force the direct stream on your client.

If your clients are using the mobile app. 
 - [swiftfin for apple](https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/swiftfin/id1604098728)
- android with the jellyfin app, they can go to client settings and change the video player to integrate player (not web based)

Hope that helps.
