  Comment: Not exactly what you asked for, but a good alternative would be to deploy your website to your own server using Shiper [Shiper](https://shiper.app). Shiper allows you to connect your own server via Shiper Self-hosted Instances. This will also make your website available to the Internet. Then, add Cloudflare cache to cache your website in the cloud.

Shiper Self-hosted Instance Documentation: https://docs.shiper.app/self-hosted

Set up CF cache with Shiper: https://docs.shiper.app/projects/domains
Navigate to step 2 and select Cloudflare DNS

Small disclaimer: I am a member of the Shiper team.
