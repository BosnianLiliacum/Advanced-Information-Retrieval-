Hello everybody,

Alex here! After the last announcement on a wording change in response to our mishap of using the words `licensed` and `unlicensed`, the team and I gave it a long hard thought over the weekend.

I thought about it when I went to bed, I thought about it in my dream, I thought about it while doing the dishes. Taking into consideration all of your feedback and suggestions, and on behalf of the team, I am happy to show you what we came up with.

* There will be no `license` or `unlicensed`.
* We will change `License Key` to `Product Key`.
* We will show a "Buy Immich" button as the call to action. The button will only be shown if the account age is older than 14 days.
* We will give you options to hide the button for 30 days or indefinitely.

https://preview.redd.it/qsrbqrn3y9ed1.png?width=886&amp;format=png&amp;auto=webp&amp;s=ce958f44efd25718bb0438f1bdb12e57c8ab0166

* We will change the price to a whole number `$100` and `$25`. So get your product key now and you can save `$0.01` ðŸ˜„
* We will add text to make our mission and intentions clear.
* We will refine the text about each option to clarify the content.

https://preview.redd.it/qbkhzb43y9ed1.png?width=1053&amp;format=png&amp;auto=webp&amp;s=cd3bb965088009ebefedd90b05536c031b4bdf2a

Last but not least, we will add a fancy `Supporter Badge` to recognize those who purchase the product. If you have any ideas for making it "shinier," let us know!

[https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ff1edb43-1838-45b9-85ed-1944645291f8](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ff1edb43-1838-45b9-85ed-1944645291f8)

Thank you again for your support and feedback. We are working on these changes and will roll out in the next release in a few days.

Have a good week!

Cheers, Immich Team
