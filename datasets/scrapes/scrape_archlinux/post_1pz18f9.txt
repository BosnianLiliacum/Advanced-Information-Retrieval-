Comment: I had a similar issue with an Intel adapter. Turned out it was not related to the hardware at all but with my ISP not providing proper IPv6 support. (Somehow Win 11 didn't care, probably didn't use IPv6 for some weird reason) 
From that experience I'd recommend checking if the issue persists after disabling IPv6 for your connection.
If this solves the issue, get in contact with your ISP and request full dual stack if possible.
If it doesn't change anything this is most likely unrelated :D
